About Heron

Heron Transportation & Logistics (Pty) Ltd | Registration Number: BW00009270828

Heron Transportation & Logistics (Pty) Ltd is a Botswana-registered company dedicated to providing efficient, reliable, and professional transportation and logistics services across Botswana and the Southern African region.

Founded in 2025, Heron was established to address the growing need for safe, timely, and transparent freight movement. The company is currently building its foundation with a modern fleet, starting with a Scania G640 truck configured for long-haul and local logistics operations.

Our Services

Freight & Cargo Transportation

  • General goods haulage (local and cross-border).
  • Distribution of raw materials and finished products.

Fleet Management & Supply Chain Support

  • Fuel management, safety monitoring, and delivery verification.
  • Advisory services on logistics efficiency for small businesses.

Logistics Coordination

  • Route optimization, scheduling, and goods tracking.
  • Partner logistics for industrial and retail clients.
